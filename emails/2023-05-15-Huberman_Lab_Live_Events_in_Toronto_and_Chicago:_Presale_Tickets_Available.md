I'm excited to announce that I'll be hosting two live events in
September 2023. The first will be in Toronto, Canada, on
September 12, followed by the second event in Chicago, Illinois,
on September 28. This is part of a speaking series called "The
Brain Body Contract (
https://click.convertkit-mail.com/27unkv238zuohv2d098f8/6qheh8h7k38kw0u9/aHR0cHM6Ly9odWJlcm1hbmxhYi5jb20vdG91ci8=
)."

The Brain Body Contract explores science and science-related
tools for mental health, physical health and performance. The
information will only partially overlap with the information I've
presented previously — most of it will be new information not yet
presented anywhere else. The talk will conclude with possibly my
favorite part of the evening, a question and answer session,
where I get the opportunity to answer your questions live.
